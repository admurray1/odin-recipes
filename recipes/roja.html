Salsa Roja

Description

A spicy fresh salsa, that relies on the flavour of the peppers and other veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 dried ancho pepper
  • 6 Roma tomatoes
  • 1/4 medium white onion, in two wedges
  • 1 (or more) serrano peppers, stemmed
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon avocado oil
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Slice the stem off the ancho pepper, then slice vertically so it lays flat.
  2. Heat a cast-iron skillet over medium heat, and add the ancho pepper. Toast for 2 minutes per side until fragrant.
  3. Remove the ancho from heat and soak in a bowl with water.
  4. With the skillet still on medium heat, add the whole tomatoes, onion wedges, and seranno pepper(s).
  5. Cook the tomatoes and peppers until brown and blistered, and the onions until charred. Make sure to manually turn the vegetables.
  6. Drain and dry the chile, then add the chile along with the vegetables, garlic, lime juice, oil, and salt to a blender. Blend until smooth.
  7. Add the fresh cilantro and pulse until combined, then season with salt to taste.
