Mashed Potatoes

Description

A recipe for creamy, rich, and flavourful mashed potatoes. A classic side dish to any meal!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and cut into large chunks
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Chopped fresh chives

Steps

  1. Place the potatoes and garlic in a pot of cold water, seasoned with 1 tablespoon kosher salt.
  2. Bring the water to a boil on high heat, then reduce and boil for 20 minutes, until the potatoes are tender. Drain and return to the pot.
  3. Mash the potatoes and garlic using a potato masher. As you mash, add the butter, milk, salt, and several dashes of black pepper. Mash until at desired consistency.
  4. Using a spatula, fold in the sour cream.
  5. Serve and garnish with a sprinkling of fresh chives.
